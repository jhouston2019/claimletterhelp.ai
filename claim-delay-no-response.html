Insurance Claim Delayed or No Response?

What This Is

If your claim has stalled or you’re not receiving clear responses, insurers may be waiting on documentation, internal review, or additional justification. Delay letters are often vague and non-committal.

Silence or delay does not always mean your claim is denied — but it does require attention.

What Happens If You Ignore It

  • Claims may be closed for inactivity
  • Deadlines may quietly pass
  • Leverage may decrease over time

How Claim Letter Help Works

Upload any recent correspondence related to the delay and get clarity on what may be causing the holdup and what steps are typically taken to move a claim forward.
