Homeowners Claim Types Supported

Property Damage

Storm damage, wind, hail, falling objects, structural damage to dwelling.

Water Damage

Burst pipes, appliance leaks, roof leaks (not flood - requires separate policy).

Fire & Smoke

Fire damage, smoke damage, lightning strikes.

Theft & Vandalism

Burglary, theft of personal property, vandalism damage.

⚠️ Homeowners Claims Requiring Attorney

System will refuse output for: Fraud allegations • EUO requests • Reservation of rights • Attorney involvement • Bad faith disputes • Claims over $50,000

Homeowners Claim Response Characteristics

Procedural Only

Fixed playbooks for information requests, denials, partial payments. No negotiation.

Scope-Limited

Maximum 20-30 lines per response. Brief, factual statements only.

Risk-Aware

Refuses output in dangerous scenarios. Recommends attorney when required.
