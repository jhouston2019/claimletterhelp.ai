When You MUST Consult an Attorney

⛔ Attorney Consultation MANDATORY For:

  • Fraud investigations – Any allegation of fraud or misrepresentation
  • Examination Under Oath (EUO) – EUO requests require attorney representation
  • Recorded statements – Recorded statements can be used against you
  • Reservation of rights – Coverage is being questioned
  • Attorney involvement – Any mention of attorneys or legal action
  • Bad faith allegations – Complex legal issues requiring attorney expertise
  • High-value claims – Personal claims over $50,000, commercial over $25,000
  • Coverage disputes – Policy interpretation or coverage questions
  • Subrogation disputes – Complex recovery or liability issues

If any of these apply, this system will refuse to generate output and recommend attorney consultation.
