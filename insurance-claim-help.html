Received an Insurance Claim Letter You Don’t Understand?

What This Means

If you received a letter or email from your insurance company about a claim, it often means they are responding to your claim, requesting information, or explaining a decision. These letters are frequently written in technical or vague language that makes it hard to understand what’s actually being said.

Many policyholders don’t lose claims because they are wrong — they lose them because they don’t respond correctly or at all.

What Happens If You Ignore It

  • Deadlines to respond may expire
  • The insurer may close or deny the claim
  • You may lose leverage to dispute the decision
