What is the Insurance Letter Response Tool?

This is a procedural, risk-aware tool that prepares structured correspondence outlines in response to insurance carrier letters. It is NOT a chatbot, NOT a negotiation tool, and NOT a legal advisor.

The tool uses fixed playbooks with predetermined sections and strict line limits. It classifies your letter, detects the phase, evaluates risk, and either prepares a procedural outline or refuses output with explanation.

Does not interpret policy language or provide coverage advice. Procedural outlines only.
