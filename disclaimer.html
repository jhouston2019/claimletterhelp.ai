Not Legal or Insurance Advice

ClaimLetterHelp is not a law firm and does not provide legal advice. It is a procedural, risk-aware, scope-limited system for preparing insurance claim correspondence only.

What This System Will NOT Do

This system will NOT:

  • Negotiate with insurers
  • Argue coverage or policy interpretation
  • Respond to fraud investigations, EUO requests, or reservation of rights letters
  • Replace professional legal representation
  • Generate output when attorney involvement is detected
  • Accept free-form narratives or "tell your story" inputs

Hard Stop Scenarios

This system will intentionally refuse to generate output in the following scenarios:

  • Fraud investigations or misrepresentation allegations
  • Examination Under Oath (EUO) requests
  • Recorded statement requests
  • Reservation of rights letters
  • Bad faith allegations or litigation threats
  • Commercial claims over $50,000
  • Any letter mentioning attorneys or legal action
  • Subrogation disputes
  • Coverage disputes requiring legal interpretation

If your letter involves any of the above, you MUST consult a licensed attorney immediately.
