Insurance Claim Denied? Here’s What the Letter Really Means

What This Is

A claim denial letter means the insurance company is stating they will not pay all or part of your claim. Denials often reference policy language, exclusions, or conditions that are difficult to interpret without experience.

A denial does not always mean the claim is validly denied or that the decision cannot be challenged.

What Happens If You Ignore It

  • Appeal or dispute deadlines may pass
  • The denial may become final
  • You may lose the opportunity to provide additional information
